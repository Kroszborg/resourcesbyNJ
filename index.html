Welcome to ResourcesbyNJ

Our Story

At ResourcesbyNJ, we started with a simple idea: to make education accessible to everyone. We believe that education is a fundamental right and should not be limited by financial barriers.

These resources play a crucial role in empowering individuals, regardless of gender or socio-economic background, to pursue their educational aspirations and contribute to community development by providing access to quality education, skills training, and learning opportunities.

Our Goal

Our end goal is to provide free access to quality resources regardless of gender or socio-economic background, to pursue their educational aspirations and contribute to community development by providing access to quality education, skills training, and learning opportunities.
